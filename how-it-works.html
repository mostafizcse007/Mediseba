Patient Journey

How MediSeba Works

From first search to follow-up care, the flow is designed to stay simple.

1

Create an account

Sign in with your email, verify the OTP, and complete your basic health profile.

2

Browse doctors

Filter by specialty, compare experience, and open each doctor profile before you book.

3

Book your slot

Pick an available date, receive a token number, and review the pending payment record.

4

Track visits and prescriptions

Use your dashboard to monitor appointments, payments, and prescriptions in one place.

MediSeba step-by-step summary

The transcript below mirrors the core patient flow so the information stays accessible without audio.

  1. Create an account, verify your email with OTP, and complete your patient details.
  2. Browse doctors, compare specialties, and choose a date that fits your schedule.
  3. Join the consultation journey, then use appointment-based chat for follow-up clarification.
  4. Open your dashboard to check payments, prescriptions, and appointment history later.
