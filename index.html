MediSeba walkthrough summary

This transcript adapts the same patient journey to the MediSeba platform so users can read the steps even without playing the video.

  1. Open MediSeba, search for a doctor by specialty or name, and compare profiles before booking.
  2. Select an available date, confirm the appointment, and receive your token and payment details.
  3. Attend the consultation and use the built-in chat if you need short follow-up clarification afterward.
  4. Review your prescription, appointment history, and payment record from your dashboard whenever needed.
